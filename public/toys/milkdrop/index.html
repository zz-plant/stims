What to expect

What is MilkDrop Visualizer?

MilkDrop Visualizer is an interactive browser-native visual toy in Stims.

How do I launch MilkDrop Visualizer?

Open the Stims player at /toy.html?toy=milkdrop to start MilkDrop Visualizer.

What kind of experience does MilkDrop Visualizer offer?

Preset-driven feedback visualizer in the lineage of Ryan Geiss's MilkDrop, with blend transitions, live source editing, and import/export flows.
