FAQ

About this toy

What is MilkDrop Visualizer?
Preset-driven feedback visualizer in the lineage of Ryan Geiss's MilkDrop, with blend transitions, live source editing, and import/export flows.
How do I start MilkDrop Visualizer?
Open the toy and press Launch. Then enable microphone or demo audio options if prompted for the most reactive visuals.
What capabilities does MilkDrop Visualizer support?
Supports live microphone input. Includes demo audio mode. No device motion input required. Uses WebGPU for enhanced effects and needs a compatible browser/device.
How does this relate to MilkDrop and projectM?
Stims is an independent browser-native visualizer built in the lineage of Ryan Geiss's MilkDrop. It is not an official MilkDrop or Winamp release, and when presets or compatibility work draw on the broader ecosystem, Stims should also credit preset authors and projectM contributors where relevant.
