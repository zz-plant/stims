Lineage

Built in MilkDrop’s lineage, with its own identity.

Stims is an independent browser-native visualizer that credits Ryan Geiss’s MilkDrop while making clear that this is its own implementation.

What carries forward

  • Preset-driven feedback visuals and fast visual iteration.
  • Browser-native access to preset browsing, editing, import, and export.
  • Clear credit to MilkDrop, Winamp-era context, and preset authors.

What changed

  • The browser is the platform, so compatibility, permissions, and performance settings are explicit.
  • The homepage explains the product before you launch it.
  • /milkdrop/ is the dedicated place for audio setup, presets, and editing.
